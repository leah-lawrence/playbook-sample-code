Getting Things Done

Daily Standups

Every morning, spend 15 minutes with your Squad to align and break bottlenecks.

Working in Sprints

To allow our teams to welcome changing requirements, we rely on short iterations called Sprints.

Create your Tracker

To help align your Tribe, follow the conventions for organizing your work.

Hills, Epics, and Stories

To stay focused and act efficiently, we organize our work in a backlog of stories, epics, and hills.

Estimate with Story Points

To help everyone understand what is achievable, we use simple estimates called Story Points.

Refine your Backlog

Continuous refinement of the backlog ensures that future work is being defined in a timely manner.

End of Sprint Playback

Come together as a broader team and share what has been done so everyone is on the same page.

Retrospectives

The team reflects on how to become more effective, then tunes and adjusts its behavior accordingly.

Celebrate with your Squad

Reflect and celebrate the shared successes with your Squad and Tribe.
